About

Block Island is a small island off the coast of Rhode Island packed full of fun for the whole family. Accessible by personal boat or our very own ferry, Block Island has been a different sort of “Island Get Away” for generations. Whether you’re coming for the beaches, the Lighthouses, or the restaurants, we assure you that this will be a vacation with memories to last a lifetime!

Lodging
Narragansett Inn

The Narragansett Inn stands on the shores of the Great Salt Pond with its marinas, restaurants and shops, with wide veranda and lawns offer spectacular views of the beautiful New Harbor.

Book Now
Spring House

The Spring House Hotel is considered one of Block Island's most elegant historic landmarks. With its wraparound veranda, mansard roof, distinctive cupola, and breathtaking view of the Atlantic Ocean and Block Island's foothills, it is no wonder why the hotel is included among New England's most desirable destinations.

Book Now
National Hotel

The National Hotel is the flagship victorian hotel on Block Island. Located in the center of downtown in Old Harbor across from the ferry landing and New England’s best beaches.

Restaurants
The Oar

Located at the Boat Basin in New Harbor on the beautiful Great Salt Pond, The Oar offers something for everyone, including the area’s best clam chowder, New England lobster rolls, burgers, and a full sushi bar.

Beachead

Becky & Tim Clark lead the Beachead team focusing on quality, innovation, and hospitality. With a passion for the culinary craft and destination locations, Tim & Becky are the proud owners of Aimer la vie Restaurant Group.
